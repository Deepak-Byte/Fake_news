How It Works

Research uses advanced artificial intelligence and machine learning techniques to analyze the credibility of news articles in real time. Here's a simple breakdown of how our system works:

  1. Text Input
    Users start by entering the full news article, headline, or excerpt they want to verify.
  2. Preprocessing
    The text is cleaned and processed using bs4 to remove noise and extract important linguistic patterns.
  3. AI Model Analysis
    The processed text is analyzed by a trained machine learning model that evaluates features commonly found in real vs. fake news.
  4. Credibility Scoring
    The model provides a verdict — "Real" or "Fake" — along with a confidence score based on the analysis.
  5. Explanation & Feedback
    We show the reasoning behind the result and invite users to provide feedback for future improvements.

While Research is a powerful tool, we encourage users to treat its results as guidance. Always cross-check with reliable sources before drawing conclusions.
